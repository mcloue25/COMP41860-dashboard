## 1.  Purpose of the Policy

This  policy  outlines  the  procedures  for  the  routine  appointment  of  newly appointed hospital consultants to the Dublin Academic Medical Centre (Mater Misericordiae University Hospital and St Vincent's Healthcare Group) as UCD Clinical Lecturers.

## 2.  Scope of the Policy

The University has agreed with Mater Misericordiae University Hospital and St Vincent's Healthcare Group that all new consultants appointed to the Dublin Academic Medical  Centre  would  be  appointed  as  adjunct  members  of  staff attached to the UCD School of Medicine and Medical Science.  For first time consultant  appointments,  the  adjunct  appointment  would  routinely  be  as  a UCD Clinical Lecturer.  Adjunct appointment to the UCD School of Medicine &amp; Medical  Science  will  be  consistent  with  the  UCD  Policy  on  appointment  of Adjunct  Staff.    Consultants  appointed  as  UCD  Clinical  Lectures  under  this process are eligible to apply for progression to:

UCD Senior Clinical Lecturer UCD Associate Clinical Professor UCD Clinical Professor via the UCD Clinical Pathway scheme which is managed by the UCD College of Health Sciences , UCD School of Medicine &amp; Medical Science.

The University Committee for Academic Appointments, Tenure and Promotions (UCAATP) at its meeting on16 January 2009 agreed the following procedures  for  appointment  of  DAMC  hospital  consultants  as  UCD  Clinical Lecturers.

## 3.  Role of the UCD School of Medicine &amp; Medical Science

The  UCD  School  of  Medicine  &amp;  Medical  Science  will  inform  UCD  HR Promotions &amp; Grading Office of all such appointments attaching the following documents for transmission to the UCAATP:

-  Copy of the appointee's CV
-  Copy of the Assessment Board's recommendation
-  Completed Post Authorisation Form

## 4.  Role of UCD HR, Promotions &amp; Grading

UCD HR Promotions &amp; Grading will bring the documentation  received  from the UCD School of Medicine &amp; Medical Science to the UCAATP for noting.

Following the UCAATP meeting UCD HR Promotions &amp; Grading will:

-  Forward the name/s of the individual/s to the Governing Authority and the Academic Council for noting
-  Will inform the newly appointed UCD Clinical Lecturers in writing of her/his appointment quoting their Personnel number.

Hospital Consultants appointed as UCD Clinical Lecturers may apply for promotion to UCD Senior Clinical Lecturer, UCD Clinical Associate Professor and UCD Clinical Professor via the UCD College of Health Sciences process which was approved by the Governing Authority on 21 March 2006.
