## Private Health Insurance Policy

Policy owner: UCD HR

Approval date and body: July 2020 UMT

## 1. Purpose

Employees of the University may join a group scheme with one of the private health insurance companies listed below, and the University will facilitate payment through payroll.  VHI Healthcare, Irish Life Health, Laya Healthcare and HSF Health Plan offer a range of healthcare plans depending on individual requirements.

## 2. Scope

To be eligible to participate in one of these group schemes, an employee must be either a permanent member of staff or a temporary or contract employee with a contract of no less than one year in duration.

## 3. Principles

Employees wishing to join any of the following group schemes should contact the relevant healthcare provider. All four companies have comprehensive websites which outline details of the various plans available.
