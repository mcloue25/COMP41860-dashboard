## Statute 29

## (Amendment of: Statute 25, Chapter 6) Universities Act 1997

## Chapter 1

## Amendment of Statute 25, Chapter 6 - The Academic Council

1. Paragraph 1 of Chapter 6 of Statute 25 is now hereby amended to read as follows:
1. There shall be an Academic Council which shall consist of the President, the Registrar, and:
3. (a) The senior member of Faculty having responsibility to the President for each academic discipline, [school or department] as the governing authority determines.
4. (c) An appropriate number of students.
5. (b) Members from what, in the opinion of the governing authority, is an appropriate rangeoflevels of other Faculty from an appropriate range of academic disciplines, and

The composition of each category of Academic Council membership will be set out in terms of reference to be approved by the Governing Authority from time to time.

Chapter 2

## Date of Commencement

This statute shall come into operation on 26 March 2020. It may be cited as Statute 29 of University College Dublin, National University of Ireland, Dublin.
